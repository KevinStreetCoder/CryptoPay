## Long-form (for the website / changelog at cpay.co.ke/changelog if we add one)

### What's new

**SasaPay deposit rail · ship-day** — We've added a brand-new way to top up your Cpay wallet from M-Pesa. Open the app, tap **Deposit**, pick the crypto you want to receive, and you get a 6-character code that you enter as the Account Number when paying our SasaPay paybill. Within 30 seconds, your USDT / USDC / BTC / ETH / SOL is credited at the live rate.

**Smarter status checks** — Previously, if M-Pesa's callback didn't reach us in time (network blips, firewall hiccups), your transaction could hang in "processing" forever. We now actively poll the M-Pesa rail every 30 seconds and resolve the saga either way · success or compensate with a clear error message.

**Friendlier errors** — Common M-Pesa rejection codes (you cancelled the prompt, took too long to enter your PIN, wrong PIN, M-Pesa offline) now show readable sentences instead of "Code 1037". The app keeps you informed.

**UI polish**
- Crypto picker on Buy Crypto page now uses a clean uniform grid instead of asymmetric cards
- Bottom-tab labels (Pay / Wallet / Me) no longer have descenders clipped on Android
- Pay tab cards reflow into 2-3-4-5 columns based on screen width

**Under the hood**
- Reconciliation case queue now has admin UI for ops to work edge-case payments
- Platform-wide safety limits (max-per-tx / hour / day caps + kill switch) layered above the float-based circuit breaker
- IntaSend payment rail wired in as a fallback to SasaPay
- Cold-wallet env wiring + air-gapped sweep policy documented
