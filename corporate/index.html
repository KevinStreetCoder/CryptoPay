What we do

Four practices, one delivery team.

Each engagement is staffed from a single in-house team. No offshoring relays, no broker layers between specification and shipped software.

01 · Mobile

Mobile applications

Native and cross-platform iOS and Android applications for fintech, retail, logistics and SME workflows. Built on modern stacks (React Native, Swift, Kotlin) with offline-first architectures designed for unreliable networks and field operations.

02 · Web

Web applications

Production-grade SaaS, internal tools and customer portals on Django, Node.js, React and Next.js. Hardened for compliance-sensitive industries including financial services, health and education, with role-based access, audit logging and observability built in.

03 · Agents

Agents and automation

How we work

Three phases. One team. Accountable end to end.

Engagements are scoped to deliver a working system, not a deck. We stay through operate.

Step 01 · Discovery

Discovery

Workshops with your operators and engineers to map the workflow, the constraints and the integrations. We leave with a written specification, a delivery plan, and a fixed-scope statement of work, reviewed by your team before any code is written.

Step 02 · Build

Build

Two-week iterations against the specification, with a working build at the end of each. CI from day one, code review on every change, and a staging environment your team can use. Your stakeholders see progress in the product, not in status reports.

Step 03 · Operate

Operate

We run the system after launch: monitoring, on-call rotation, incident response, and ongoing iteration against your roadmap. Knowledge transfer is built into the engagement so your team can take over fully, partially, or never. Your call.
